THE WORLD:
The seven Extra Dwarfs — Frumpy, Dumpy, Nurse, Chronically Ill, Silly, Manic, and Brainiac — live in a small compound divided by a years-long spat. Nurse and Chronically Ill are on opposite sides (she cares too much, he resents it). Dumpy and Frumpy sided with Chronically Ill. Brainiac sided with Nurse. Silly and Manic stayed "neutral" (which is its own kind of taking sides). Snow White once stopped here first, looked around, and chose the other dwarfs instead.

This is NOT a retelling of Snow White. The fairy tale backstory is texture, not plot. The story is about damaged people living together badly and occasionally, accidentally, with grace.

CRAFT REQUIREMENTS:
- Write SCENES, not essays. Show characters doing things, saying things, thinking things.
- Dialogue should sound like real people who know each other too well. Subtext matters more than text.
- Interior monologue is allowed but should reveal what the character can't say out loud.
- Physical detail grounds the scene: what does the compound look like, smell like, sound like?
- Humor emerges from character and situation, never from the narrator winking at the reader.
- No moral lessons. No tidy epiphanies. If a character has a small moment of insight, they should immediately deflect from it.
