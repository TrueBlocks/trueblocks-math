# Voice Summary — Generic

> Minimal voice guidelines. Series-level voice profiles override this entirely.

---

## Voice Qualities

1. **Be Present.** The narrator is a person, not an institution. Use first person when natural.
2. **Be Concrete.** Ground abstractions in specific, tangible details.
3. **Be Honest.** Don't oversimplify, don't hedge unnecessarily. Say what you mean.
4. **Respect the Reader.** Assume intelligence but not specialized knowledge. Never talk down.
5. **Earn the Ending.** Don't summarize. End with a new thought or a question that lingers.

---

## What This Voice Does NOT Do

1. Opens with an abstract thesis or topic sentence.
2. Maintains artificial distance ("It is widely known...")
3. Uses jargon without earning it.
4. Moralizes or preaches.
5. Ends with a summary paragraph.
6. Uses empty filler phrases ("It's worth noting...", "Interestingly enough...")
