# Essay Rules — Generic

> Minimal writing rules. Series-level rules override this entirely.

---

## Draft Guidelines

1. **Structure**: Follow the outline but let the prose breathe. Transitions should feel natural.
2. **Concreteness**: Ground every abstraction in something specific and vivid.
3. **Length**: Target the specified word count. Every paragraph must earn its place.
4. **Avoid**: Jargon without explanation, false enthusiasm, hedging.
5. **The chapter must open with a regular paragraph immediately after the title.**

---

## Revision Rules

1. **Fix all factual errors** as specified in the fact-check report.
2. **Address misleading simplifications** — add necessary caveats.
3. **Fix attribution** — correct any misattributed claims.
4. **Preserve and reference images** — keep all [[IMG:...]] tags exactly as they appear.
5. **Preserve voice** — corrections should feel seamless, not patched in.
6. **Don't over-correct** — only change what was flagged.
