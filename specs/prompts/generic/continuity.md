Check the draft against the outline and internal consistency. Report:

1. **Plot Continuity**
   - Events that contradict the outline or earlier chapters
   - Timeline inconsistencies (time of day, day of week, seasons)
   - Cause-and-effect gaps — things that happen without setup

2. **Character Continuity**
   - Characters who appear without introduction or disappear without explanation
   - Personality or voice shifts that aren't motivated by the story
   - Physical descriptions that contradict earlier mentions
   - Knowledge consistency — does the character know things they shouldn't (or forget things they should know)?

3. **Setting Continuity**
   - Spatial inconsistencies (room layouts, distances, geography)
   - Objects that appear or vanish between scenes
   - Environmental details that contradict the established world

4. **Pacing and Structure**
   - Scenes that drag or feel rushed relative to their importance
   - Missing transitions between scenes
   - Chapter ending — does it create forward momentum?

5. **Continuity Summary**
   - Overall assessment: CLEAN (minor issues only), REVISE (significant issues), or BROKEN (structural problems)
   - List of specific changes needed, in order of severity

FORMAT: Markdown. Quote the problematic passages directly. If everything checks out, say so — don't invent problems.
