A Mystery Starring Three Young Protagonists

•	They are visiting their grandparents in Philadelphia, PA for a few weeks in the summer

•	Grandparents live in Old City / Society Hill section of Philadelphia having just moved there from their Gladwyne home after all their kids have finally moved out.

•	The mystery has something to do with Philadelphia history ala National Treasure.

•	The kids live in Seattle with their divorced mother (not the grandparent’s daughter).  She lives high on a hill in Seattle ala Free Willie movie with beautiful views of the bay.

•	The kids fly alone on the plane to Philadelphia International Airport.

•	This is the fourth year they’ve flown out for the summer and visited.

•	Their grandfather works as a docent in the Carpenter’s Hall building in Independence Park.

•	After the initial introduction each chapter in the book focuses on a different part of the city: Rittenhouse Square, Queen Village, Old City, Society Hill, Fairmont, Germantown, Northern Liberties, Fishtown, Nicetown, Overbrook Farms, Manyunk, Frankford, Chestnut Hill, University City, etc.

•	In the Seattle chapters which are an intro to their family, we meet each of the three kids in a separate chapter.  (14 year old girl, 13 year old boy, 10 year old girl).
