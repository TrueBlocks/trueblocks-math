## *Everything Is a Rate of Change: Essays on the Hidden Mathematics of Everyday Life*

---

The organizing principle: **start with your own body, expand outward through your kitchen, your neighborhood, the living world, the things humans build together, the things we find beautiful, and finally the cosmos — then end at the frontier, where the questions are still open.** Read the part titles in sequence and you'll feel the arc: from the intimate to the infinite, from the known to the unknown.
