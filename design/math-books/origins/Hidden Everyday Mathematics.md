## How to Read This Table

- **The Everyday Experience** is the hook — something every reader has done or seen.
- **The Hidden Math** is the punchline — the surprising mathematical structure lurking beneath.
- **Surprise Factor** (★ to ★★★★★) rates how unexpected the math connection is to a non-specialist.
- **Essay Seed** is a one-line angle for a future Asimov-style essay.

## Selection Criteria

The best topics for this series share three traits:

1. **Universal experience** — the reader has personally encountered it
2. **Non-obvious math** — the connection to formal mathematics is surprising
3. **Depth available** — the topic can sustain a 2,000–4,000 word essay that goes from "here's what you already know" to "here's what you didn't realize you were doing"

Topics 1–2 are already written. The remaining 33 are proposed.
